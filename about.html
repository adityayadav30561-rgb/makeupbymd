Meet Deepika

Certified makeup artist, nail designer, and the heart behind every transformation at the studio.

An Artist's Hands,
A Bride's Trust

Deepika Singh is a certified makeup artist trained at Geetanjali Academy, with two years of dedicated experience in bridal, engagement and party makeup across Kanpur.

She is passionate about creating flawless, personalised looks that enhance natural beauty and boost confidence for every special occasion. For Deepika, makeup is never a mask — it is a celebration of the woman wearing it.

Alongside makeup, she crafts couture nail art and custom press-on sets — because elegance should reach all the way to your fingertips.
